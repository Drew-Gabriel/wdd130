History

Founded in 1990, Whitewater Rafting Adventures has been bringing thrill-seekers together for over 30 years. Our experienced guides and top-notch equipment ensure a safe and exciting experience for all our guests.

Throughout the years, we've expanded our reach to several rivers, each offering its own unique challenges and breathtaking landscapes. Join us on the next great adventure!
